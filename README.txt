# DeinterioCafe Website

## Overview
Welcome to DeinterioCafe, an innovative and stylish interior design company. Our website showcases our services, portfolio, and allows potential clients to contact us directly. This repository contains all the code and assets used to build and maintain the DeinterioCafe website.

## Features
- **Responsive Design:** The website is fully responsive and optimized for different devices, including desktops, tablets, and mobile phones.
- **Service Descriptions:** Detailed information about our services and what makes us unique in the interior design industry.
- **Portfolio Gallery:** A showcase of our previous projects and designs.
- **Contact Form:** Allows visitors to get in touch with us easily.
- **About Us:** Information about the company, its history, and its mission.

## Technologies Used
- **HTML5 & CSS3:** For structuring and styling the website.
- **JavaScript:** For interactive elements and form validation.
- **PHP:** For backend processing, including handling the contact form submissions.
- **Bootstrap:** For responsive design and layout.
- **FontAwesome:** For icons used throughout the site.
- **Flaticon:** For additional icons representing services.
